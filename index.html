I am a first second year graduate student in Computer Science at UC San Diego.

I am working on problems in online education with Professor Scott Klemmer. Currently, I am developing tools to achive multiple learning objectives using peer groups in online discussions. Details as we go along!

In Winter 2015, I am TAing the intriguing undergraduate Human-Computer Interaction Design class.


During Summer 2014, I developed efficient data structures for integrity checks on outsourced databases with my mentor Arvind Arasu at Microsoft Research Redmond. In my first year at UCSD, I worked with Professor Steven Swanson at the Non-Volatile Systems Lab.

Before beginning graduate school, I worked at the Advanced Technology Group at NetApp towards building new storage products using techniques in low-latency networking, security and more. Even before (phew!), I graduated from BITS Pilani in India with an undergraduate degree in Computer Science.


I also help organize Annual Alumni Research Talks and Annual Alumni Award for Excellence in Computer Science at BITS Pilani. If you're a current student or a recent alumnus, you should definitely check those out. The last date to apply for the 2014 award was October 27th.

I am currently helping out with the Association of Indian Graduate Students at the university towards organizing events for incoming students and the Indian and university community at large. Your small donations fuel our samosa and chai parties!

Advanced Technology Group, NetApp
July 2011 - May 2013
As a Member of Technical Staff, I worked in teams of 2 or 3 engineers to design, implement and measure the performance of prototypes. We worked to scale up the vaulting capabilities in the existing OS code and to reduce the failover time (drastically!) in a cluster with high availability configuration. The ideas and techniques demonstrated were taken up by multiple product teams and led to patent application and internal papers.

Along with team members, I was selected for Honorable Mention in 'Innovation' and 'Teamwork' categories in semi-annual CTO awards.
